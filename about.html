Campus Milestones

About NexEvent

NexEvent is the primary coordination node connecting student bodies with regional engineering hackathons, sports championships, and cultural meets.

Our Mission

To provide an automated, fluid, and robust event administration framework that removes bureaucratic delays and lets students focus on preparing, building, and excelling in their respective fields.

Our Vision

To establish a standard campus ecosystem where cross-department collaborations, extracurricular sports, and artistic exhibitions thrive under a modern student portal, connecting over 10,000+ students.
